This website is not very subtle. On reflection, when you are presenting and selling your skills, subtelty is not beneficial. Instead, you should be bold, be brash, and be loud.

While easy to use, the system is also easy to break with bugs galore. However, that isn't unusual for a project implementing new technologies such Mermaid. In additiom, refactoring more of the code to be data driven is slowly fixing several lingering issues and helping me address a violation of the DRY principle among my radiobuttons. All in all, I am pleased with what my efforts have produced.
